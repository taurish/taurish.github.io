A bit about our study

Our study is called "CMU Longitudnal Age Prediction Study (CLAPS)". With the hype of neural networks and deep learning, while everyone is busy "creating and training" models that are hypothesized to be inspired by the human brain, our study set out to test whether the brain actually gets trained overtime. We intend to test whether a human brain can be trained to perform tasks that we expect a neural networks to do. The primary task at hand is the age prediction from voice. Additionally, we are also testing the same theory for the prediction of weight and height from voice.
